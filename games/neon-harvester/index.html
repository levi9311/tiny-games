About Neon Harvester

Neon Harvester is an idle clicker and incremental game that puts you in control of a sprawling data harvesting network in a cyberpunk darknet. Start by manually clicking the glowing neon core to extract data fragments ($DF), then spend your earnings on automated facilities — data probes, scan crawlers, quantum decryptors, darknet nodes, and neural networks — each one generating passive income around the clock. As your empire grows, watch your $DF counter climb from thousands to millions to billions, with satisfying milestone celebrations at every breakthrough. The game saves your progress automatically and even calculates offline earnings, so your network keeps producing while you're away. With its mesmerizing neon visuals, combo system, achievement challenges, and deeply addictive progression loop, Neon Harvester is the perfect free browser game for multitaskers and number-crunching fans alike. Built as a pure HTML5 game with zero downloads required, it runs smoothly on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. Start your data empire today and see how far the rabbit hole goes in this free online idle game.

How to Play

  • • Click the neon core to harvest data fragments ($DF)
  • • Buy automated facilities for passive income
  • • Upgrade and expand your data harvesting network
  • • Unlock 12 achievements as you progress
  • • Progress saves automatically — even earns while you're offline
