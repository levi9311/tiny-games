Neon Merge - Cyberpunk Merge Game

Neon Merge is a free browser-based merge game with a cyberpunk neon aesthetic. Drop glowing neon orbs onto the grid and watch matching colors merge and evolve into more powerful forms. Strategy meets reflexes in this addictive casual puzzle game.

🎮 How to Play

  • 🔸 Tap or click to drop a neon orb into a column
  • 🔸 Two matching orbs touching will automatically merge into the next level
  • 🔸 The evolution chain: Gray → Cyan → Green → Yellow → Orange → Pink → Magenta → Red → White
  • 🔸 Don't let orbs stack above the red danger line!
  • 🔸 Plan your drops — merged orbs apply gravity and can chain-merge
  • 🔸 Higher level merges = exponentially more points

🔮 Features

  • ✦ Pure HTML5 Canvas — works on any modern browser
  • ✦ Responsive — optimized for phone, tablet, and desktop
  • ✦ 9-level neon evolution chain with unique colors
  • ✦ Merge particles and floating score effects
  • ✦ Local best score tracking
