About Neon Memory

Neon Memory is a cyberpunk-themed card matching game that challenges your short-term memory and pattern recognition skills. A grid of face-down cards hides pairs of matching symbols — flip two at a time and find all the pairs to clear the board. With four difficulty levels ranging from a gentle 4×3 Easy grid to a brain-burning 6×5 Expert board, there's a challenge for every skill level. In Free Play mode, take your time and try to complete the board in as few steps as possible. In Time Trial mode, race against the clock to match all pairs before time runs out. Built as a free HTML5 browser game, Neon Memory runs instantly on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile phone — with zero downloads, no installs, and no sign-ups required. The satisfying 3D card flip animations, neon glow effects, and star rating system make every successful match feel rewarding. Play Neon Memory online now and give your brain a neon-lit workout.

How to Play

  • • Click or tap a card to flip it over and reveal the symbol
  • • Flip a second card — if both symbols match, they stay revealed
  • • If they don't match, both cards flip back face-down
  • • Find all matching pairs to clear the board and win
  • • Try to finish in fewer steps for a higher star rating
  • • In Time Trial, beat the clock before time runs out!
