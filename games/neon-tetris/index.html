About Neon Tetris

Neon Tetris is a free online cyberpunk-themed block puzzle game that reimagines the classic Tetris experience with stunning neon visuals and modern gameplay mechanics. Guide falling tetromino blocks onto a 10×20 grid, rotate and position them to complete horizontal lines, and watch them shatter in dazzling particle effects. Built as a pure HTML5 browser game, Neon Tetris requires no downloads, no installs, and no sign-ups — just open and play instantly on any device, whether desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. The game features a deep combo system that rewards consecutive line clears with score multipliers up to 3x, an energy-based power-up system with three unique abilities (Ghost Mode, Burn Row, and Slow Motion), a daily challenge mode with seeded random sequences so every player faces the same block sequence each day, and 10 unlockable achievements to track your progression. Your high scores, achievements, and lifetime statistics are automatically saved to your browser's localStorage, so your progress persists between sessions. With its eye-catching cyberpunk aesthetic — including a dark grid background, glowing neon blocks in seven distinct colors, scanline overlays, data rain animation, and glass-morphism UI panels — Neon Tetris delivers the perfect blend of nostalgic block puzzle gameplay and futuristic visual flair. Whether you're a Tetris veteran chasing high scores or a casual player looking for a quick free browser game, Neon Tetris on PlayPixelCraft offers endless entertainment.

How to Play

  • • Desktop: Arrow keys to move (← →), Up arrow to rotate, Down arrow for soft drop, Space for hard drop, P to pause
  • • Mobile: Use the on-screen virtual buttons or swipe gestures — swipe left/right to move, swipe up to rotate, swipe down for soft drop, tap for hard drop
  • • Complete horizontal lines to clear them and earn points
  • • Consecutive line clears build combos for score multipliers (up to 3x)
  • • Fill the energy bar by clearing lines to unlock random power-ups
  • • Try the Daily Challenge for a fixed sequence and compete with friends
  • • Unlock 10 achievements and track your lifetime statistics

Scoring System

  • • 1 Line: 100 × Level · 2 Lines: 300 × Level · 3 Lines: 500 × Level · 4 Lines (Tetris): 800 × Level
  • • Soft Drop: +1 per cell · Hard Drop: +2 per cell
  • • Combo multiplier: 2-combo ×1.5, 3-combo ×2, 4+ combo ×3
  • • Level up every 10 lines cleared — speed increases each level
